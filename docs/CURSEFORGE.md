## Description

# 📦 Amazing™ — Earth's Blockiest Store

**Ever wished someone would just… deliver the diamonds to you?** Now they do.

Amazing™ adds a full online megastore to Minecraft 1.21.1. Browse a catalog of **140+ products**, click Buy, and watch an actual **delivery van drive up to your base** — a uniformed Amazing courier hops out, walks the package to your door, and hands it over. Rip it open and enjoy. Five stars.

Works in **singleplayer and multiplayer** (install on both server and client).

## 🏙️ Amazing City
Create a world with the **Amazing City** world type and spawn into an infinite procedural metropolis: road grids with lane markings, furnished houses, apartment towers, parks, street lamps, corner ATMs — and Amazing **warehouses stacked floor-to-ceiling with packages** (yes, you can open them; no, we won't tell HR). Citizens with unique skins and names live here, order things, chat with you, and judge your delivery times.

*Servers: set `level-type=amazing:city` in server.properties.*

## 🚚 Drivable vehicles
Deploy your **Amazing Van** or the speedy **Amazing Roadster** and drive with WASD — smooth boat-style physics that feel great even on servers. Spinning wheels, steering, exhaust smoke, a passenger seat, and a horn (**H**). HONK.

## 🛒 The catalog
Press **O** to shop: 9 departments from Tools & DIY to Grocery to Furniture & Tech, plus **Prime Exclusives** (elytra, totems, shulker boxes, your own van). Search, browse, shift-click for 4-packs. Deliveries arrive in ~30–60 seconds by van; villagers and citizens get their own ambient deliveries too.

## 💵 MineBank
A real economy: every player has a **dollar account**. Withdraw cash at **working ATMs**, deposit banknotes, sell emeralds, wire money to friends. **Amazing Prime** costs $20 per 30 in-game days and unlocks the exclusive deals. e-banking included, fees not (for now).

## 📱 AmazingPhone X
One phone, six apps: **Amazing** (shop + live order tracking), **MineBank**, **GPS** (coordinates, heading, waypoints), **Quests**, **Radio** — which streams *real internet radio stations* (Ogg Vorbis) into the game — and **Phone**, for calling your Mom. She worries.

## 🗺️ Quests
Amazing couriers are always hiring: courier runs, warehouse supply runs, and express review gigs. Get paid in dollars, earn a free Prime Card after 3 quests, and your **own van** after 6. Employee of the month material.

## 🛋️ Working furniture & tech
Sittable chairs, tables, togglable lamps, and a TV that actually turns on. Sold flat-packed via the catalog, assembly not required.
